Maldives


The Maldives is a tropical paradise located in the Indian Ocean, known for its pristine white sandy beaches, stunning coral reefs, and luxury overwater bungalows. It is a perfect getaway for honeymooners, adventure seekers, and anyone looking for a tranquil escape.

Top 5 Attractions in the Maldives

  1. Malé – The vibrant capital city with historic mosques and bustling markets.
  2. Banana Reef – A world-renowned diving spot filled with exotic marine life.
  3. Manta Point – The best place to witness majestic manta rays up close.
  4. Vaadhoo Island – Famous for the magical “Sea of Stars” phenomenon.
  5. Hulhumalé Beach – A serene beach known for its turquoise waters and golden sands.

Must-Try Foods in the Maldives

  • Mas Huni – A delicious breakfast dish made with tuna, coconut, and onions.
  • Garudhiya – A traditional fish soup served with rice, lime, and chilies.
  • Fihunu Mas – Grilled fish seasoned with Maldivian spices.
  • Bondibaiy – A sweet coconut-flavored Maldivian rice pudding.
  • Hedhikaa – A variety of Maldivian snacks like fish cakes and samosas.
